Chicken Sandwich

Description

This Chicken Sandwich is the perfect balance of crispy, tender chicken served on a soft, toasted bun. Whether you're craving a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner, this sandwich delivers on both flavor and texture. The chicken is seasoned to perfection and can be pan-fried, grilled, or even breaded for extra crunch.

Top with your favorite toppings like fresh lettuce, tomato, pickles, and a creamy mayo or spicy mustard sauce. Add a slice of cheese for extra richness, or make it a healthier version with avocado. This versatile sandwich can be easily customized to your taste!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 sandwich buns
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard (optional)
  • 2 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 2 slices tomato
  • Pickles (optional)
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika on both sides.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is fully cooked and golden brown on the outside. If you’re using a thermometer, the internal temperature should reach 165°F (75°C).
  3. If you’re adding cheese, place the slice of cheddar cheese on top of the chicken breasts in the last minute of cooking and cover the skillet to melt the cheese.
  4. While the chicken cooks, toast the sandwich buns in a separate pan or in a toaster until golden and crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise and mustard together. Spread the mayo-mustard mixture on the inside of the toasted buns.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, assemble the sandwich: Place a piece of chicken on the bottom bun, then layer with a slice of tomato, lettuce, and pickles (if using). Top with the other bun.
  7. Serve immediately with a side of fries or chips, and enjoy!
